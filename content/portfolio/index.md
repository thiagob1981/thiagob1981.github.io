## Agricultural Data Platform

Built a universal property database from 30+ heterogeneous government and industry sources for the Brazilian agribusiness sector. Implemented a multi-stage matching cascade (geospatial, document-based, and fuzzy text matching) to harmonize rural property records at scale.

**Stack**: Databricks, Delta Lake, PySpark, Spark SQL, Power BI
